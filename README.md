# 🚗 ChargePilot AI

## AI-Powered Electric Fleet Management & Optimization Platform

ChargePilot AI is a full-stack intelligent fleet management platform designed to help EV fleet operators monitor vehicles, analyze trips, predict range, evaluate risks, and optimize charging decisions using AI-driven insights.

The platform combines a modern React frontend with a FastAPI backend and AI-powered analysis services to provide a complete EV fleet intelligence solution.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📊 Fleet Dashboard

- Real-time fleet analytics
- Total vehicles tracking
- Total trips monitoring
- Average battery analysis
- Average trip distance
- Fleet utilization calculation

---

## 🚘 Vehicle Management

- Manage EV fleet vehicles
- Track vehicle information
- Monitor battery conditions
- Maintain fleet visibility

---

## 🛣️ Trip Analysis

AI-based trip evaluation using:

- Battery percentage
- Battery capacity
- Distance
- Vehicle load
- Temperature
- Traffic conditions
- Weather conditions

Provides:

- Predicted driving range
- Trip success probability
- Risk evaluation
- Estimated energy requirement
- Charging recommendations

---

## 🤖 AI Trip Optimization

Integrated AI analysis provides:

- Energy requirement estimation
- Range prediction
- Charging suggestions
- Trip feasibility analysis
- Operational recommendations

---

## 🎮 What-If Trip Simulator

A simulation engine allowing fleet operators to test different scenarios.

Users can change:

- Battery level
- Distance
- Load
- Temperature
- Traffic

The simulator calculates:

- Expected range
- Trip completion probability
- Risk score
- Charging requirement

---

## 🔋 Charging Intelligence

Charging analysis system provides:

- Available energy calculation
- Required energy estimation
- Recommended charging percentage
- Trip feasibility decision

---

## 🛠️ Fleet Health Monitoring

Fleet health module analyzes:

- Battery condition
- Vehicle utilization
- Operational performance

---

## 🧠 Predictive Maintenance

Maintenance monitoring module designed for:

- Vehicle health tracking
- Maintenance recommendations
- Future AI-based failure prediction
